How to Play

Game Overview

HUNTED is a real-time GPS-based pursuit game with players split into two teams: Hunters and Runners. Runners navigate through progressively smaller zones to reach their final target while evading Hunters who track and attempt to catch them by taking their photo.

Teams

Hunters

Track Runners in real-time and catch them by taking their photo. You MUST keep your app open at all times.

Runners

Navigate through zones to reach your final target while staying hidden. Each Runner has their own unique final target zone.

For Runners

Your Mission: Navigate through progressively smaller zones until you reach your final target zone to win.

How Zones Work:

  • Each Runner has a unique final target location, randomly placed within the play area
  • You'll discover your target through a series of nested zones that get smaller as you progress
  • When a new zone first appears, it starts locked for a short time
  • Once unlocked, you can capture it to reveal the next zone
  • Each newly revealed zone will also start locked and you must wait for it to unlock before it can be captured
  • A countdown at the top of the screen shows when the current zone unlocks, plus how many zones remain

Location Pinging:

  • Your location is shared with Hunters while you have the app open.
  • You can see where other Runners and Hunters have pinged - use this intel wisely!
  • If you're already inside a locked zone, stay hidden and wait for it to unlock before opening the app again
  • Warning: Taking too long to reach your final target gives Hunters more time to catch other Runners, who then join the hunt for you!

Getting Caught: If a Hunter takes your photo, report it using the "I've Been Caught" button. You'll become a Hunter and join the pursuit.

Being a successful Runner is about staying hidden and timing your movements carefully. Lay low, watch the map, and move strategically.

For Hunters

  • Keep your app open at all times
  • View the play area and track Runner locations on your map in real-time
  • See where Runners have pinged and predict their movements
  • Catch Runners by taking their photo (share it in your group chat as proof!)
  • Work together - caught Runners become Hunters, growing your team
  • The play area center is typically set to your starting location, so Runners are hiding nearby

Winning

Runners Win Individually: Each Runner who reaches their final target zone is marked as having "won" - multiple Runners can win!

Hunters Win Together: If all Runners are caught before any reach their final target, Hunters win as a team.

Safety Tips

  • Always observe traffic laws and safety regulations
  • Play only in public spaces - no trespassing
  • Runners are caught by photo, not physical contact
  • Respect others' privacy - keep photos within your game group
  • Stay aware of your surroundings at all times
  • Keep communication methods available for emergencies
